Our Story

Welcome to I CUT Whitecross Barbers — a premium barbershop at the heart of Clerkenwell, London. We've built our reputation on precision, care, and an uncompromising standard of grooming that keeps our clients coming back.

Located on Whitecross Street, one of London's most vibrant and community-driven streets, we serve clients from Clerkenwell, Barbican, Old Street, Farringdon, Shoreditch and beyond. Whether you're a city professional, a local resident, or visiting the area — you'll always be welcomed.

We believe a great haircut is more than just a trim. It's a full experience. From the moment you sit in the chair, every detail is considered: your hair type, your face shape, your lifestyle, and your personal style.

Our Story

Welcome to I CUT Whitecross Barbers — a premium barbershop at the heart of Clerkenwell, London. We've built our reputation on precision, care, and an uncompromising standard of grooming.

Located on Whitecross Street, we serve clients from Clerkenwell, Barbican, Old Street, Farringdon, Shoreditch and beyond. Whether you're a city professional or a local resident — you'll always be welcomed.

We believe a great haircut is more than just a trim. It's a full experience. From the moment you sit in the chair, every detail is considered: your hair type, face shape, and personal style.

At I CUT, we don't just follow trends — we craft looks that belong to you. Our mission is to ensure every gentleman feels completely renewed.

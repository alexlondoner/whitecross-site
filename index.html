About Us

Welcome to I CUT Whitecross Barbers — a premium barbershop at the heart of Clerkenwell, London. Located at 136 Whitecross Street EC1Y 8QJ, we've built our reputation on precision, care, and an uncompromising standard of grooming.

We believe a great haircut is more than just a trim — it's a full experience. From the moment you sit in the chair, every detail is considered: your hair type, your face shape, your lifestyle, and your style goals.

Our services range from classic cuts and hot towel shaves to full luxury grooming packages — all delivered with the skill and attention you deserve. Whether you're a regular or visiting for the first time, you'll always leave looking and feeling your best.

Cancellation Policy

We value your time and ask that you value ours. Please read our cancellation and no-show policy carefully before booking.

Cancellations

We require a minimum of 24 hours notice for any cancellation or rescheduling. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice may result in a cancellation fee or loss of any deposit paid.

No-Shows

Clients who do not show up without prior notice will be charged 100% of the service fee. Repeated no-shows may result in being unable to book future appointments.

Late Arrivals

If you are more than 15 minutes late, we may need to shorten your service or reschedule your appointment.

Same-Day Bookings

Same-day appointments must be confirmed directly via WhatsApp or phone before attending.

To cancel or reschedule please contact us at least 24 hours in advance via WhatsApp at 020 3621 5929 or call us directly. Thank you for your understanding.

After Hours Booking

After hours appointments (7PM–9PM) require direct confirmation.

A surcharge applies. Please contact us directly to confirm availability and pricing.

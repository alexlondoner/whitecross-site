Booking Policy

Cancellation · No-Show · Late Arrival

Last updated: March 2026

📋 Overview

At I CUT Whitecross Barbers, we take pride in offering a premium experience for every client. To ensure we can serve everyone fairly and efficiently, we ask all clients to read and respect the following booking conditions.

By making a booking with us — whether online, via WhatsApp, or in person — you agree to the terms outlined on this page.

❌ Cancellations

We require a minimum of 24 hours notice for any cancellation or rescheduling of an appointment.

Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice may result in:

— A cancellation fee being charged
— Loss of any deposit or prepayment made
— Restrictions on future bookings

⏰ To cancel or reschedule, please contact us at least 24 hours before your appointment.

🚫 No-Shows

A no-show is when a client fails to attend their scheduled appointment without prior notice.

Clients who do not show up will be charged 100% of the booked service fee. This charge will be applied to any card or payment method on file.

Clients with two or more no-shows may be required to pay a non-refundable deposit for all future bookings, or may be unable to book further appointments.

📱 If something comes up, always let us know — we understand life happens.

⏱ Late Arrivals

We ask all clients to arrive on time or a few minutes early for their appointment.

If you arrive more than 15 minutes late, we may need to:

— Shorten your service to fit the remaining time
— Reschedule your appointment to a later slot
— Treat the booking as a no-show if we cannot accommodate you

This is to ensure fairness for all clients booked after you.

⚡ Same-Day Bookings

Same-day appointments are subject to availability and must be confirmed directly with us before attending.

Please contact us via WhatsApp or phone to check availability before making your way to the shop. Walk-ins are always welcome but cannot be guaranteed a specific time slot.

💬 WhatsApp us to confirm a same-day slot: +44 7879 553312

💳 Payments & Deposits

Payments can be made in-store by cash or card. For certain bookings, a prepayment or deposit may be required to secure your appointment.

Deposits are non-refundable in the event of a no-show or cancellation with less than 24 hours notice.

For priority online bookings, payment is collected at the time of booking via our secure Stripe payment link.

↩️ Refunds

If you are dissatisfied with your service, please let us know before leaving the shop so we can make it right. We are committed to ensuring every client leaves happy.

Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis and are at the discretion of the barbershop.

We do not offer refunds for completed services where the client was satisfied at the time of the appointment.

Need to cancel, reschedule or have a question about your booking? Reach us directly — we're always happy to help.
